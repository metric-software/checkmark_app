## What We Collect

Checkmark collects **only anonymized benchmark data**. Before any upload, the app strips fields that could identify you.

**Anonymized data we may upload:**
- Component models (CPU/GPU names), RAM capacity, storage type, OS version
- Benchmark results (numbers only)
- Relevant Windows/driver settings (with paths/usernames removed)

**We do NOT collect:**
- Names, emails, usernames, hostnames
- Device serial numbers, MAC addresses, IP addresses
- File paths containing personal information
- Any unique device identifiers

## No Accounts

Checkmark does not require user accounts. No personal authentication data is collected or stored.

## How We Use Data

Anonymized benchmark data is used to:
- Provide comparisons and leaderboards
- Improve software quality
- Produce aggregated statistics

We may publish or share anonymized aggregate statistics. These contain no personal data.

## Your Choices

You can run benchmarks locally without uploading any data.

## Data Location

Our infrastructure is located in the EU (Finland and Germany).

## Children

Checkmark is not directed to children under 16.
